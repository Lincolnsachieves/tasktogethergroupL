About TaskTogether

TaskTogether helps teams and individuals capture tasks, organize work on MY PROJECTS, and chat in real time—right in the browser.

Built with simplicity in mind, TaskTogether brings together your to‑dos, MY PROJECTS, and conversations so you can focus on what matters. No installs. No sign‑ups required to explore the demo. When you’re ready, create an account in seconds and keep working.

Our principles

  • Clarity over complexity—features should be obvious.
  • Speed matters—every action should feel instant.
  • Collaboration first—tasks and chat live together.
