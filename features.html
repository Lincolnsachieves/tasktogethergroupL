Features

MY PROJECTS

Kanban-style lists for moving work forward with clarity.

Break work into lanes like To Do, In Progress, and Done. Drag cards, assign owners, and track status at a glance across MY PROJECTS.

Inbox

Capture ideas instantly across devices and tabs.

Jot down tasks the moment they pop up. Your Inbox is the front door for all your ideas—move items to MY PROJECTS when ready.

Chat

Real-time discussion that stays with your tasks.

Discuss tasks where they live. Conversations remain attached to the work so decisions aren’t lost in separate apps.
